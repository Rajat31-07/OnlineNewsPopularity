Media companies and content creators struggle to predict which
articles will gain traction online. Popularity is influenced by multiple
factors like keywords, sentiment, timing and social signals which
makes manual estimation unreliable.​

Extracted from Mashable articles published over a period, including metadata, content features and social engagement metrics.​
